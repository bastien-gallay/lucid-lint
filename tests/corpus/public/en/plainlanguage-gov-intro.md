## Welcome!

Plain language – content that is clear and easy to understand – is critical to helping the public to make sense of their obligations and benefits. These guides are designed to help web managers and content strategists to understand why and how to create content that readers can understand.

The Plain Writing Act of 2010 established the requirement that content for the public is written for its specific audience. These guides help you to understand how to create, design and test content so your specific audience understands it.
